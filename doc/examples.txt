########
Examples
########

The following examples will guide you through the functionality of *aristopy*.
The first example shows a straightforward application to introduce you to the
basic model setup and the notation of *aristopy*.
More advanced features are presented with a larger model in the second example.
The last example model shows some functionality that is certainly helpful when
working with models that utilize solar components (solar thermal
collectors or photovoltaic modules).
